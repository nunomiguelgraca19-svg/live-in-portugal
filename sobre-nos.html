About Us

We’ve been through the process ourselves. That’s why we built this.

Why this exists

Moving to a new country is exciting. But the practical side — finding the right neighbourhood, understanding local prices, navigating Portuguese bureaucracy — can be overwhelming, especially when you don’t know anyone on the ground.

We built Portugal Expat Home because we went through this ourselves. We know what it’s like to look at a map of a country you’ve never lived in and try to figure out where you’d be happy. We know what it’s like to deal with Finanças, AIMA, and landlords who don’t speak English.

Our goal is simple: give you the information you need to make a good decision, and be there to help if you want someone handling the details.

How we work

We’re not a real estate agency. We don’t sell properties and we don’t take commissions from landlords or developers. That means our recommendations are always in your interest, not someone else’s.

Our free matching tool uses real market data to suggest the best areas for your profile. If you then want hands-on help — property visits, NIF registration, contract signing — we offer those as transparent, fixed-price services.
